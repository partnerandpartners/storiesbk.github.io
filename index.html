Stories

Bookshop and storytelling lab in Brooklyn.

We believe stories can change the world.

A good story ignites our imagination, curiosity, and passion for learning.

Stories is a family business, co-founded by Maggie Pouncey and Matt Miller. In our shop in Brooklyn, we will sell a beautiful collection of children’s books for ages zero through young adult, with a section for parents, too; and we will teach a rich catalogue of storytelling classes for kids in our adjacent lab space. Our programs will be offered after school, on weekends, and over the summer.
